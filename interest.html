Research Interests

My research focuses on developing scalable algorithmic frameworks for analyzing large-scale and high-dimensional data while preserving their properties. I am particularly interested in probabilistic techniques and sketching methods that bridge theoretical guarantees with practical machine learning systems. My broader research area includes the followings:

  • Similarity Search problems.
  • Algorithms for Machine Learning.
  • Scalable Algorithms for Large-Scale Data.
