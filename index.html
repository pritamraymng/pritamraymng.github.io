About

Hi, I am Pritam Ray, from Maynaguri, a city located at the foothills of the Himalayas in Jalpaiguri district, West Bengal, India. Currently, I am a PhD student in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering, IIT Hyderabad. Before joining IITH, I relished two beautiful years (2023-2025) at the great IIT Madras for my M.Tech. Studies in Industrial Mathematics & Scientific Computing at the Department of Mathematics.

This webpage presents my academic background, research interests, teaching experience, and achievements. My goal is to contribute to scalable algorithmic foundations bridging theory and real-world data.
